## Description

Antworks Studio is a small game studio composed of three Unicamp students united by their common taste for games.

## History

In 2012, after a lot of hardships with various tools and no experience, the three co-fouders found Unity, the game engine, and started developing their first pojects. Their first game came only in 2013, Conexus, which is a result of a local game jam.
Today, Antworks Studio is preparing for new projects and ideas, always in search of challenging but fun games.
